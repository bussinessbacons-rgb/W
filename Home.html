Artificial Intelligence is rapidly becoming one of the most powerful tools in modern education. From personalized tutoring systems to smart classroom analytics, AI is helping educators deliver more effective learning experiences.

This issue of Annals of the Phoenixx explores how AI is transforming education across the world and what it means for the future of students and teachers.

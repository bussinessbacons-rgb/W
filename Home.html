Introduction

Artificial Intelligence is becoming an important part of modern education. Many students now use AI-powered tools to help them study, research, and understand difficult lessons. These tools provide personalized learning experiences and allow students to learn at their own pace.

In this digital magazine, we explore how AI is transforming classrooms, supporting teachers, and helping students achieve better learning outcomes.

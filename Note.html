Editor's Note

Education has always evolved alongside technology. Today, artificial intelligence represents one of the most significant changes in how knowledge is delivered and accessed.

AI tools can support teachers, assist struggling students, and open new opportunities for personalized learning. However, these technologies also require responsible use and careful guidance.
